<h1>Times</h1>
<p>1 hour = 60 minutes</p>
<p>1 minute = 60 seconds</p>
<p>1 hour = 3600 seconds</p>
<p>Duration  = EndTime - StartTime</p>

<h1>Units of Length</h1>
<p>1 cm = 10 mm</p>
<p>1 m  = 100 cm</p>
<p>1 km = 1000 m</p>
<p>1 sqm = 10,000 sqcm</p>

<h1>Quardilaterals</h1>
<p>Four sides and four angles</p>
<p>Squares : 4 sides and 4 90 degree angles</p>
<p>Rectangle → opposite sides equal, 4 × 90°</p>
<p>Rhombus → 4 equal sides, no 90°</p>
<p>Parallelogram → opposite sides parallel & equal</p>
<p>Trapezoid → exactly 1 pair of parallel sides</p>

<h1>Perimeter</h1>
<p>Rectangle: P = 2 × (l + w)</p>
<p>Square: P = 4 × s</p>
<h1>Area</h1>
<p>Rectangle: A = l × w</p>
<p>Square: A = s²</p>


<h1>Fractions</h1>
<p>a / b : a -> numerator; b -> denominator</p>
<p>Compare same numerator and same denominator</p>

<h1>Sets & Venn diagrams</h1>
<h2>Union (at least one)</h2>

<h2>Intersection (both)</h2>
The overlap region — counted once only
<h2>Neither</h2>
Total − (A ∪ B) = Neither

<h1>Calendar facts</h1>
<p>1 year = 12 months = 365 days (366 leap)</p>
<p>1 week = 7 days</p>
<p>1 day = 24 hrs</p>
<h1>Month lengths</h1>
<p>31 days: Jan Mar May Jul Aug Oct Dec</p>
<p>30 days: Apr Jun Sep Nov</p>
<p>28/29 days: Feb</p>
